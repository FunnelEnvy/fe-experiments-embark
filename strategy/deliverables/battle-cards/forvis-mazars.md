# Battle Card: Forvis Mazars

**Last Updated:** February 2026
**Threat Level:** Low

## In One Sentence

A global professional services firm formed by merging BKD and Dixon Hughes Goodman with Mazars International, offering assurance, tax, and consulting with a trademarked "Unmatched Client Experience" claim.

## Their Positioning

Forvis Mazars positions as "Assurance | Tax | Consulting" with "Unmatched Client Experience" as their registered trademark and primary differentiator. The brand represents a three-way merger: BKD (Midwest heritage), Dixon Hughes Goodman (Southeast heritage), and Mazars Global (international network). They claim 15+ proprietary tools and emphasize innovation, adaptability, and partnership-oriented advisory.

## What's Actually True

The "Unmatched Client Experience" trademark is real and registered. The global network through Mazars provides legitimate international reach. Regional CPA heritage (BKD, DHG) provides local market depth. The FORsights publication and proprietary tools signal research investment. The firm exists and is operational.

## Where They're Weak

- "Unmatched Client Experience" is a trademark, not a proof point. No published NPS, attrition rate, retention rate, or client satisfaction data backs the claim. The trademark creates an expectation their execution doesn't visibly deliver on.
- Triple-merger integration (BKD + DHG + Mazars) creates brand confusion. Buyers may not know which firm's culture, processes, and people they are actually hiring.
- Consulting is tertiary after assurance and tax. Advisory talent and attention compete internally with the primary revenue lines.
- The merger itself is still relatively recent, meaning internal cultural alignment is a work in progress.

## Where We Win

- **Quantified proof vs. trademarked claim.** Embark's NPS 93 directly counters "Unmatched Client Experience." Our client experience is measured. Theirs is trademarked. The question for buyers: which one would you rather bet on?
- **Advisory focus.** Embark's entire business is advisory. Forvis Mazars' advisory is a tertiary service line after assurance and tax.
- **Clear identity.** Embark is one firm with one culture. Forvis Mazars is three firms merging under a new brand.
- **Client retention data.** 91% client retention, 9% attrition. Forvis Mazars publishes no comparable data.

## Where We Lose

- When the buyer needs assurance + tax + consulting under one roof. The bundle model has structural convenience.
- When the buyer values global network access through Mazars International.
- When the buyer's operations span US and international markets.

## Landmines

Their sales team will lead with "Unmatched Client Experience" as a trademarked differentiator. Counter: "What's your NPS score? Our NPS is 93. Our client retention is 91%. 'Unmatched' is a word. These are numbers."

They may position the merger as "best of three firms." Counter: "Mergers take years to integrate. Which firm's people and processes will show up on your engagement? We've built a single, cohesive team: 90% employee approval, 9% attrition."
