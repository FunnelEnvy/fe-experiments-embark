# Battle Card: Cherry Bekaert

**Last Updated:** February 2026
**Threat Level:** Medium

## In One Sentence

Full-service CPA and advisory firm offering audit, tax, and consulting under one roof with deep technology partnerships (Sage, Microsoft Dynamics 365) and industry specialization across 10+ verticals.

## Their Positioning

Cherry Bekaert positions around "tailored, cutting-edge strategies" with extensive industry coverage. Their competitive advantage is the bundle: audit + tax + advisory from a single firm, combined with dedicated technology implementation services (Sage 100c, Sage X3, Dynamics 365, Sage Intacct). They also offer CFO Advisory services with middle market survey insights and fractional CxO services that directly compete with Embark's Office of the CFO.

## What's Actually True

The one-stop-shop model is real and valued by buyers who want fewer vendor relationships. Technology partnerships are extensive and established (Sage, Dynamics 365, Sage Intacct). Industry coverage across 10+ verticals provides depth. Recruiting & staffing solutions add a talent delivery dimension. The CPA firm model is inherently more comprehensive for buyers needing assurance + advisory.

## Where They're Weak

- CPA firm model means audit independence rules constrain advisory scope for audit clients. If Cherry Bekaert audits you, they cannot advise you on certain matters.
- Advisory is a secondary revenue stream to audit/tax. Advisory talent may be less specialized than a pure-play firm like Embark.
- Jack-of-all-trades positioning dilutes the specialization signal. They do everything, but the question is depth vs. breadth.
- No published culture or satisfaction metrics.

## Where We Win

- **Pure advisory without conflicts.** Embark has no audit independence constraints. Advisory scope is unrestricted. For companies whose auditor is also Cherry Bekaert, switching advisory to Embark removes the independence limitation.
- **Big 4 Alumni talent depth.** Embark's talent model is built around senior Big 4 Alumni practitioners. Cherry Bekaert's advisory team competes internally with audit and tax for senior resources.
- **Culture proof.** NPS 93, 9% attrition, 91% retention. Cherry Bekaert publishes none of these metrics.
- **Case study outcomes.** 17 published case studies with quantified results vs. Cherry Bekaert's survey-based insights.

## Where We Lose

- When the buyer wants a single firm for audit + advisory + tax. Cherry Bekaert's bundle reduces vendor count and simplifies procurement.
- When the buyer needs technology implementation on Sage or Dynamics 365 platforms. Cherry Bekaert has dedicated practice lines for these platforms that Embark does not match.
- When the buyer is in government or non-profit. Cherry Bekaert has dedicated practices in these sectors; Embark explicitly excludes them.

## Landmines

Their sales team will lead with the one-stop-shop advantage: "One firm for audit, tax, and advisory." Counter: "If we also advise you, independence rules limit what we can tell you. Your auditor should audit. Your advisor should advise without restrictions. That's why independence matters."

They may highlight technology implementation depth on Sage and Dynamics 365. Counter: "We focus on NetSuite, Workday, and five other technology partners. If you're on Sage or Dynamics 365, ask us whether those are the right platforms for your growth trajectory."
