# Battle Card: Opportune

**Last Updated:** February 2026
**Threat Level:** Low

## In One Sentence

Houston-based energy advisory firm combining consulting, investment banking, and outsourcing with 20 years of exclusive energy sector focus.

## Their Positioning

Opportune positions as "Consulting. Investment Banking. Outsourcing." with the tagline of "leading global energy advisory firm." Their core claim: "No other firm offers our breadth and depth of energy-related services." The 20-year energy specialization across three delivery models (consulting, investment banking, outsourcing) is their primary differentiator.

## What's Actually True

The energy specialization is genuine: 20-year track record, Houston headquarters (energy capital), multiple managing directors in specialized energy practices, and investment banking capability that is unique among competitors in this set. The three-model approach (consulting + investment banking + outsourcing) is a legitimate structural differentiator within the energy vertical.

## Where They're Weak

- Extreme energy sector concentration limits relevance for non-energy companies. If you are not in oil & gas, this firm is likely not a real option.
- Investment banking arm creates different economic incentives than pure advisory.
- Limited visibility in non-energy sectors. No evidence of cross-industry capability or case studies.
- Smaller scale than multi-sector competitors. Estimated 200-400 employees.
- Houston-centric presence creates geographic limitations.

## Where We Win

- **Multi-sector coverage.** Embark serves 19 industries from 26 offices. Opportune is energy-only from Houston.
- **Non-energy engagements.** Any engagement outside oil & gas is Embark territory.
- **Scale and coverage.** 26 offices vs. Houston-centric presence. $149M revenue vs. undisclosed.
- **Case study breadth.** 17 published case studies across 15+ industries, including energy (Presidio Petroleum: 10x well-count growth, oil & gas E&P automation).

## Where We Lose

- When the buyer is an energy company needing deep commodity accounting, reserve engineering, or energy-specific restructuring.
- When the buyer needs investment banking combined with consulting. Opportune's investment banking arm is unique in this competitive set.
- When the buyer's PE sponsor has energy portfolio companies that need Opportune's specialized bench.

But we also serve 18 other industries, which matters when your portfolio is diversified."
