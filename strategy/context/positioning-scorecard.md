## Quick Reference

# Embark - Positioning Quick Reference

**One-liner:** Financial advisory and consulting for mid-market and PE-backed companies that accelerates financial clarity and execution by deploying Big 4 Alumni consultants who stay, perform, and care -- backed by an NPS of 93 and 9% attrition.
**Category:** Financial advisory and consulting
**Primary persona:** CFO (PE-backed or mid-market in transition)
**Primary differentiator:** Happy Works -- quantified culture proof (NPS 93, 9% attrition, 91% retention) that no competitor can replicate

### What We Do
Embark is a financial advisory and consulting firm that embeds Big 4 Alumni advisors alongside client teams to handle complex financial reporting, audit readiness, transactions (M&A, IPO), digital transformation, and Office of the CFO functions. They execute, not just advise -- project-based, retainer, or interim/fractional engagements with no long contracts. Embark is not a CPA firm and does not perform audits or tax services.

### Who We Serve
Mid-market to enterprise companies ($5M+ revenue) across 19 industries, with concentration in PE-backed portfolio companies, organizations in transition (M&A, IPO, CFO departure), and high-growth companies outpacing their finance infrastructure. 26 US offices, Dallas HQ.

### Key Personas
| Persona | Core Problem | Our Value |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| CFO (PE-backed/mid-market) | Reporting gaps, audit deficiencies, and transaction complexity exceeding lean team capacity | Senior Big 4 Alumni advisors who embed alongside the team, close gaps, and stay until the job is done |
| COO (scaling mid-market) | Manual processes and fragmented systems that don't scale with revenue | Hands-on process redesign, ERP implementation, and change management -- execution, not just strategy |

| CHRO/CPO (organizations in transition) | Finance talent gaps that can't be filled fast enough through traditional recruiting | Interim/fractional leadership deployed in weeks, not months, from consultants who integrate well |
