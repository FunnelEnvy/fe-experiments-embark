## Company Overview

Embark is a financial advisory and consulting firm serving mid-market to enterprise organizations across 19 industries from 26 US offices. Founded in 2010 by Paul Allen in Dallas, TX, the firm reported $149M in revenue in 2023 and has received strategic investment from Parthenon Capital. Embark positions itself around a "Happy Works" philosophy, claiming that consultant satisfaction drives client outcomes, and staffs engagements with Big 4 alumni and industry veterans.

## Constraints

### Regulatory
- **Texas State Board of Public Accountancy Compliance**: Embark is not a CPA firm and must not use language that implies CPA services or audit capabilities.
  - Restricted terms: "accounting" in service names, "CPA"/"CPAs" in titles/marketing, "Big 4 CPAs" (use "Big 4 Alumni"), "Outsourced Accounting" (use "Outsourcing"/"Back Office Outsourcing"), "Technical Accounting" (use "Complex transaction analysis and advisory"), "Expert"/"Specialist" (use "Advisor"/"Consultant"/"Skilled advisor"), "audit facilitation" (use "Audit support and readiness"), "accounting firm", "accounting services", "audit services" in marketing context
  - Required terms: "Big 4 Alumni", "Licensed professionals", "Advisors"/"Consultants", "Financial Advisory and Reporting Services (FARS)"
  - Required disclaimer: "Embark is not a CPA firm." (appears in website footer)

- **ESG compliance deadlines**: Evolving regulatory requirements (SEC climate disclosure, California climate laws) creating urgency
- **Revenue recognition complexity**: ASC 606 compliance requirements for growing companies
