## Language Constraints

**Texas State Board of Public Accountancy compliance (December 2024 update):**

### Must NOT use:
- "accounting" in service names (use "financial reporting & advisory" instead)
- "CPA" or "CPAs" in any titles or marketing materials
- "Big 4 CPAs" (use "Big 4 Alumni" or "Former Big 4 Professionals")
- "Accounting & Reporting" as service name (use "Financial Advisory & Reporting Services (FARS)" or "Financial reporting & advisory")
- "Outsourced Accounting" (use "Outsourcing" or "Back Office Outsourcing")
- "Technical Accounting" (use "Complex transaction analysis & advisory" or "GAAP & IFRS Advisory")
- "Financial Accounting" (use "Finance & reporting advisory")
- "Purchase Price Accounting" (use "Acquisition reporting assistance" or "Purchase Price Allocation Advisory")
- "Lease Accounting" (use "Lease reporting compliance" or "ASC 842 Support")
- "Carbon Accounting" (use "GHG reporting & compliance" or "Emissions reporting")
- "Accounting experts" (use "Experienced professionals" or "Experienced advisors")
- "Accounting expertise" (use "Proven advisers" or "Trusted advisers")
- "Expert" as qualifier (use "Advisor", "Consultant", "Leader", "Skilled advisor")
- "Specialist" (use "Advisor" or "Skilled advisor")
- "Audit facilitation" (use "Audit support & readiness" or "Audit preparation")
- "Industry expertise" (use "Industry experience")
- "FAAS" / "Financial Accounting Advisory Practice"
- "Finance & Accounting Team Continuity" (use "Finance & operations team continuity" or "Team continuity")

### Must USE:
- "Big 4 Alumni" (not "Big 4 CPAs" or "Ex-Big 4 CPAs")
- "Licensed professionals" (not "CPAs")
- "Advisors" or "Consultants" (not "Experts" or "Specialists")
- "Financial Advisory & Reporting Services (FARS)" or "Financial reporting & advisory" (not "Accounting & Reporting")
- "Complex transaction analysis & advisory" or "GAAP & IFRS Advisory" (not "Technical Accounting")
- "GHG reporting & compliance" (not "Carbon Accounting")
- "Audit support & readiness" or "Audit preparation" (not "Audit facilitation")

- All marketing and sales material must include: *Embark is not a CPA firm.*
